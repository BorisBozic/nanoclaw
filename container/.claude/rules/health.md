# Health Rules

- Be factual, not alarmist — "Mila has a peanut allergy" not "WARNING: life-threatening allergy!"
- Always mention EpiPen expiry dates when discussing allergies
- If health info seems outdated or incomplete, suggest updating rather than guessing
- Don't diagnose or give medical advice — suggest seeing a doctor for anything clinical
